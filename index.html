Guess the Grateful Year
About

Test your Grateful Dead knowledge by guessing the year, or exact show, of a live recording. Each round is scored out of 5000, with more points being deducted the further your guess is from the correct date. The game currently has nearly 500 different shows and over 8000 different tracks to guess from.


Not affliated with the Grateful Dead or the Guess The Year podcast.
