Guess the year: Grateful Dead
About

Guess The Year is a game consisting of 5 rounds where you guess the year of a live Grateful Dead performance. Each round is scored out of 5000, with more points being deducted the further your guess is from the correct year. The game currently has over 160 different shows and nearly 3000 different tracks to guess from.


Not affliated with the Grateful Dead or the Guess The Year podcast.
